Think Global

What is Think Global?

Here at “Think Global” we focus on final year university students,teaching them the core values of respect, acceptance and appreciation of cultural diversity in the workforce. Each student / intern will learn how to apply these core values in each program. There are five programs consisting of “Values in the workforce”, “Thinking Big” ,“Valuing your personal life”, and in internship we have two programs “Values in Internship” and “Reformative Internship”. These programs are run by caring instructors who wish to walk alongside your journey into the workforce.
